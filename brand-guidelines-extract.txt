1. Brand Philosophy & Positioning
Cube Financial Group operates as an elite boutique wealth management firm and family office advisory. The brand serves ultra-high-
net-worth individuals, multi-generational families, business owners, and corporate executives who demand sophisticated structural
clarity over complex financial ecosystems.
Our core positioning is rooted in the theme: "Solving Complexity" (מפצחים את המורכבהמורכבות מפצחים את המורכבאת  את המורכבות). Wealth is multi-dimensional, consisting
of  tax  structures,  retirement  horizons,  asset  allocations,  and  generational  transitions.  The  cube  represents  this  structured
dimensionality—stable, highly organized, and logically complete.
CORE BRAND ATTRIBUTES
ATTRIBUTE BRAND EXECUTION STRATEGY
Premium & Sophisticated We employ classic editorial typography, generous white space, and restrained deep corporate

Trustworthy & Stable Visual layouts emphasize structural balance, heavy grid alignment, and symmetric geometry to
project multi-generational permanence.

2. Master Logo System & Clear Space
The approved Cube Financial Group logo is the definitive, unalterable visual signature of the brand. The 3-
dimensional geometric cube represents organized capital, integrated asset layers, and structural alignment. This
symbol must always be treated as a single cohesive unit.
CLEAR SPACE RULE
To preserve visual integrity and high-end positioning, the master logo requires strict isolation from competing
elements. The absolute minimum clear space surrounding all sides of the logo must equal the dimension of exactly
two rows of the cube pattern, denoted as X.
Mandatory Isolation Formula
Let  Hcube be  the  total  height  of  the  cube  symbol.  The  required  clear  space  boundary  margin  Mcs is
mathematically defined as:
Mcs = 0.25 × Hcube

5. Correct and Incorrect System Usage
Consistency is key to maintaining a premium brand identity. The following examples outline mandatory design
boundaries. Any deviation dilutes the firm's reputation for stability and institutional precision.
MANDATORY CORRECT EXECUTIONS
✔ Rule 1: Monochromatic and Vector Alignment
The logo must always sit flat on light ivory, pure white, or solid deep navy backgrounds. All typographic
elements must run perfectly parallel to horizontal document margins.
✔ Rule 2: Complete Symbol Cohesion
The cube symbol must remain perfectly intact as a single block. The layout must match the original asset
dimensions without modification.
PROHIBITED VIOLATIONS
✘ Violation 1: Component Fragmentation
Never detach, offset, floating-animate, or pull out individual smaller squares from the primary cube cluster.
The cube represents structural unity; fracturing it breaks the brand code.
✘ Violation 2: Extraneous Financial Imagery
Never introduce secondary industry icons. The use of umbrellas (insurance), shields (security), compound
charts, arrows, coins, houses, or traditional scales of justice is strictly banned. The cube is the brand.
✘ Violation 3: Unauthorized Distortions
